## 👋 Radio Communication Volunteers of Saint Joseph County Indiana

🎉 Congrats on finding our organization's profile on GitHub!

We are an active community of Amateur Radio Volunteers interested in providing service to help our local community. We provide radio communication services for parades, marathons, festivals and other community events.
